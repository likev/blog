Oct 23, 2014

动量方程

\[ \frac{\text{d}u}{\text{d}t}- \left( f+u\frac{\tan\phi}{a} \right) \upsilon = -\frac{1}{a \cos\phi}\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{\partial \lambda}+F_{\lambda} \] \[ \frac{\text{d}\upsilon}{\text{d}t}+ \left(f+u\frac{\tan\phi}{a}\right)u = -\frac{1}{\rho a} \frac{\partial p}{\partial \phi}+F_{\phi} \]

静力学平衡

\[ g=-\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\partial p}{\partial z} \]

质量守恒

\[ \frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t}=-\frac{1}{a \cos\phi} \left[ \frac {\partial }{\partial \lambda} \left( \rho u \right)+ \frac{\partial}{\partial\phi} \left( \rho\upsilon\cos\phi \right) \right] -\frac{\partial }{\partial z} \left(\rho w \right) \]

热力学第一定律

\[ C_{p}\frac{\text{d}T}{\text{d}t}-\frac{1}{\rho}\frac{\text{d}p}{\text{d}t}=Q \]

\[ p=\rho RT \]
